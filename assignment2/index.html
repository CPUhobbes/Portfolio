About Me

I was born in Orange, California and moved to Texas when I was 5 years old. I lived in the Dallas area for a few years then moved to El Paso, Texas. After high school, I went to college at the University of Texas at Austin persuing a degree in Chemistry with a focus on Computation Chemistry. While there I also received my Elements of Computing Certification.

After college, I worked for various companies in the chemical industry. At this time, I work for the Texas State Fire Marshal's Office as a forensic scientist. In the past I have been attempting to transition to becoming a backend developer. I have developed a few websites and I am a arduino/raspberry pi enthusiast. Currently, I am attending the UT Austin Bootcamp to further my skills and prepare myself for a new role.
